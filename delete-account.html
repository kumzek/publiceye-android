Delete Your Account & Data

App: PublicEye  |  Developer: Kumar Abhishek  |  Last updated: June 21, 2026

You can permanently delete your PublicEye account and all associated data at any time, directly from within the app. There is no need to email us or wait for a manual review.

How to Delete Your Account from the App

  1. Open the PublicEye app and sign in.
  2. Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the action when prompted.

Your account and all associated data listed below will be permanently deleted immediately. This action cannot be undone.

Alternative: Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer not to use the in-app option, you can request deletion by emailing kumar.abhishek90@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 days.

What Gets Deleted

The following data is permanently deleted when you delete your account:

Data TypeAction
Account email and display nameDeleted immediately
Authentication record (Firebase Auth)Deleted immediately
User profile document (Firestore)Deleted immediately
Profile photo URLDeleted immediately
Upvote historyDeleted immediately

What May Be Retained

To preserve the integrity of public civic reports for the wider community, the issue reports (title, description, category, photos, GPS location, status) you submitted may remain visible in the app in anonymized form — your name and account identifier are removed and replaced with "Deleted User." If you want a specific report removed entirely, include the report ID(s) in your email request or contact us before deleting your account.

Backups and logs: Aggregated analytics data and system logs (which do not personally identify you) may be retained for up to 90 days for security and operational purposes, after which they are automatically purged.
