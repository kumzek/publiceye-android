Privacy Policy

App: PublicEye  |  Last updated: June 21, 2026  |  Developer: Kumar Abhishek

PublicEye is a civic issue reporting app that lets citizens report local problems (such as potholes and road damage) with photos and GPS location. We take your privacy seriously and only collect what is necessary to make the app work.

1. Information We Collect

When you use PublicEye, we collect the following:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you sign in with Google or create an email/password account.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates at the time you submit a report. Location is collected only when you actively submit a report — not continuously in the background.
  • Photos: Images you choose to attach to a report. Photos are uploaded to Firebase Cloud Storage and linked to your report.
  • Report content: Title, description, and category of issues you submit.
  • Usage data: App usage statistics and crash reports collected automatically via Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and display your civic issue reports on the app's map and feed.
  • To identify you as the author of a report and enable features like upvoting.
  • To send you push notifications about updates to your reports or reports near you (only if you grant permission).
  • To enforce usage limits (10 reports per user per 24-hour period) to prevent spam.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app stability via crash reports and analytics.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Your data may be shared in the following limited ways:

  • Other app users: Reports you submit (including photos, location, and description) are visible to all signed-in users of PublicEye.
  • Google / Firebase: We use Google Firebase for authentication, database, storage, push notifications, analytics, and crash reporting. Firebase's privacy policy applies: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure (Firestore database and Cloud Storage) hosted in the Asia South region. We use Firebase security rules to ensure users can only modify their own data. Connections to Firebase are encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

5. Data Retention

Your account and reports are retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Section 7 below). After deletion, your data will be removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Push Notifications

PublicEye may send push notifications to alert you about activity on your reports. You will be asked for permission before any notifications are sent. You can withdraw this permission at any time in your device's Settings.

7. Your Rights and Account Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.

To delete your account, use the Delete Account option inside the app (Profile → Delete Account). This will permanently remove your account, reports, and photos from our systems.

For any other data requests, contact us at: kumar.abhishek90@gmail.com

8. Children's Privacy

PublicEye is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
